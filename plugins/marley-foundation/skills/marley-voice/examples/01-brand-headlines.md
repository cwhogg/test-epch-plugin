# Marley brand-voice headlines

The 18 canonical brand-voice headlines from the HUb personality deliverable (May 2022). These were produced by Marley's brand agency working from the five personality descriptors and are the highest-fidelity examples of the Marley voice in short-copy form.

Use these as cadence/tone anchors — not as copy to reuse verbatim.

---

We're hyper-focused on hypertension.

Don't just do "something" about your hypertension, do the best thing.

Maybe take off the blood pressure cuff before you high five us…

If anyone claims to manage hypertension better, take it with a grain of salt. No wait. A hypertension specialist team. (Your blood pressure could drop a few points just reading that).

You can use the best way to manage hypertension, or you know, some other way. For your appendix, whatever. But for your heart, do the best.

A program that fits into your life, to push hypertension out of it.

Even the cost will lower your blood pressure.

Just knowing you have the best team in place will bring your BP down.

You inherited a set of silverware and hypertension from your grandmother. You don't have to keep both.

If you wake up in the middle of the night worried about your blood pressure, we'll be right there with you.

A plan that's 100% more effective at treating hypertension than "hoping it will go away."

Second best is totally fine for floor cleaners. For your hypertension, not so much.

Stress affects high blood pressure. We help you reduce both.

All we think about is hypertension (we're a blast at parties).

There are lots of things in life you can't control. Blood pressure isn't one of them.

Less like managing your hypertension and more like wrestling it to the ground.

We make treating hypertension almost as easy as getting it in the first place.
