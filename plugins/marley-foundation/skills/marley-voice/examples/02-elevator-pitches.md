# Marley elevator pitches (short / medium / long)

Three lengths, three options each. From the HUb personality deliverable. These are the canonical Marley elevator pitches — the way the company explains itself across all audiences.

---

## Short pitch

### Option 1 — "Your Goal is Our Goal"
At Marley Medical, your goal is our goal, to beat high blood pressure. And with us, you succeed because we bring you a comprehensive clinical care team of specialists who listen, coordinate, encourage, monitor and answer questions, 24/7. We also bring medication and equipment to your door, handle insurance, and lower costs. It's easy. It's the best. And it's the future of healthcare for people with high blood pressure.

### Option 2 — "Single Best Approach"
Marley Medical has the single best approach to managing high blood pressure – an integrated, virtual team of experts, spanning all disciplines, working together, for you. We listen and come up with a plan, together. We deliver medication and equipment, as well as answers and encouragement, 24/7. It's convenient, affordable, team-based, and the future of successful care for people with high blood pressure.

### Option 3 — "Meet Your Expert Team"
Meet your expert team to manage high blood pressure. Marley Medical is the future with a clinical care team of experts here 24/7 to answer, monitor and motivate. Meds and equipment are delivered to your door, and insurance and Medicare are handled for you. It's the convenient, and best, way to get your blood pressure back in line.

To beat high blood pressure, ideally, you'd have a full, integrated team of specialists on hand continuously to monitor, guide and motivate. We've brought that proactive team approach to your living room with Marley Medical. Our complete, expert clinical care team is at your fingertips, 24/7, to help you succeed. We also lower stress by handling insurance and Medicare with clear and transparent copays, deliver medication and equipment to your door, and lower costs. There's never been a better way to treat hypertension. It's coordinated, convenient care that's leading everyone to a lower-blood pressure future.

### Option 3 — "What A Relief"
Having high blood pressure can stress you out — which ironically, causes high blood pressure. At Marley Medical, we take the stress out of things by giving you 24/7 virtual care support from a multi-disciplinary, world class team of experts. They give you coordinated care, monitoring and a boost when needed. And while we're lowering your stress level and your blood pressure, we also lower costs. Even insurance or Medicare is handled for you, and we make sure you understand any copays. It's the future of healthcare and the best thing you can do for yourself. That's a relief.

**What to learn from these:**
- The three lengths are not three different messages — they are the same message at three depths.
- "The future of healthcare" / "the best way" / "24/7" / "coordinated, convenient, continuous" appear across all three.
- Even the long version stays plainspoken — no jargon, no hedging.
- Every pitch ends with a confident, often-sentence-fragment close: "That's a relief." / "It works." / "You have a new team for a healthier life."
