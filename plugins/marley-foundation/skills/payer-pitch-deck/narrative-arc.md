# Payer pitch deck — canonical narrative arc

Extracted from `payer pitch decks/`, primarily the **BCBS FL deck (March 2024)** and the **Marley Medical Payer Deck Feb 2024** — both of which represent the matured 2024 payer narrative. Earlier decks (UHC Accelerator May 2023, OSF March 2024) follow the same arc with payer-specific adjustments.

The Marley payer pitch is a **3-act structure** within a topics-for-discussion framing:

1. **Marley Medical Overview** — what we are, the problem we solve, our model, our evidence
2. **How Marley Works with Payers** — fit to payer challenges, contracting structures, easy-to-work-with framing
3. **Appendix: Clinical Data Deep Dive** — outcome data in depth, geographic expansion
