# Payer pitch deck — canonical narrative arc

Extracted from `payer pitch decks/`, primarily the **BCBS FL deck (March 2024)** and the **Marley Medical Payer Deck Feb 2024** — both of which represent the matured 2024 payer narrative. Earlier decks (UHC Accelerator May 2023, OSF March 2024) follow the same arc with payer-specific adjustments.

The Marley payer pitch is a **3-act structure** within a topics-for-discussion framing:

1. **Marley Medical Overview** — what we are, the problem we solve, our model, our evidence
2. **How Marley Works with Payers** — fit to payer challenges, contracting structures, easy-to-work-with framing
3. **Appendix: Clinical Data Deep Dive** — outcome data in depth, geographic expansion

## Act 2 — How Marley Works with Payers

### Slide 16 — Marley meets payer needs (2-column slide)
Left column: what Marley is designed to address —
- A focus on an expensive patient population
- Comprehensive care model with high engagement
- Ability to control a significant portion of medical spend
- Ability to selectively refer to in-person care, when needed

Right column: the payer challenges we solve —
- **Engagement** — Up to 30% of an MA membership is not engaged. We can bring them back.
- **Star Ratings** — Based on chronic disease control and NPS. We improve both.
- **MLR** — Polychronic patients are 70% of the cost. We reduce specialty and in-patient spend.

### Slide 21 — Patient selection criteria
Four criteria:

3. **Patient is not engaged in care** — no PCP claim in 12–18 months, no cardiometabolic Rx in last 6 months
4. **Patient is failing management** — 2+ ED visits in last 12 months, 1+ inpatient episode in last 12 months

**Visual spec:**

3. **Submit Claims Based on Milestones** — agreed clinical milestones (enrollment, active in month, reaches goal, Stars/HEDIS metrics)

## Payer-specific customization

Each deck is **prepared for a specific named payer**. Customize:
- Cover slide (payer name + date)
- Any state-specific evidence to the payer's primary states
- "Easy contracting" slide: reference BCBS contracts if pitching another BCBS plan; reference Anthem if pitching Anthem affiliate, etc.
- B2B pipeline / current payer-relationships slide reflects the payer's competitive context
