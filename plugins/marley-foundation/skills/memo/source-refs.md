## Redactions applied

In both example files:
- Specific cash position dollar amounts and burn-rate scenario numbers ↓ generalized
- Named investor mentions ↓ removed
- Personnel detail beyond senior team ↓ summarized
- Patient-cohort SDOH detail ↓ aggregated

Per the redaction policy: structure, voice, and section conventions preserved; concrete confidential figures removed.
